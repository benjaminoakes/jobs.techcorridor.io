Support Engineer

Technical Iowa City, Iowa

MetaCommunications is looking for a Technical Support Engineer to provide enterprise level support for our customers. The perfect candidate will blend technical knowledge, curiosity, and strong communication skills to their role. This client-facing position is responsible for troubleshooting network, hardware and software issues and providing customers with world-class support.

Responsibilities

  • Provide prompt and accurate feedback to customers and resolution of issues
  • Take ownership of customer issues reported and seeing problems through to resolution
  • Research, diagnose, troubleshoot and identify solutions to resolve customer issues via phone, screen share, live chat, and email
  • Become an expert of all areas of our software products
  • Execute new customer deployments and upgrades for current customers
  • Follow procedures for escalating unresolved issues to the appropriate internal teams
  • Work with our engineering team to resolve advanced issues
  • Ensure proper recording and closure of all issues
  • Document troubleshooting and problem resolution steps with knowledge base notes and articles
  • Participate in customer training
